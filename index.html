Our Story

A Taste of
Beirut in Richmond

At Shawarma Beirut, we bring the vibrant flavours of Lebanese street food to Richmond, Ontario. Every dish is prepared fresh daily with authentic recipes passed down through generations.

From our perfectly spiced shawarma to our signature garlic sauce and creamy hummus — each plate is a journey to the heart of Beirut. Our platters are served with fragrant rice, golden potatoes, crisp salad, fresh pita bread, and all the fixings that make a meal feel like home.
